About Me

Web Developer!

My name is Rita Chinwendu Chiaha, and I am a beginner in the exciting world of web development. I am passionate about creating beautiful, functional, and user-friendly websites that leave a lasting impression. I discovered my love for web development recently and it's been exicting. I was immediately drawn to the idea of being able to bring my creative ideas to life on the internet. Since then, I have been on an incredible journey of learning and refining my skills. Throughout my web development journey, I have acquired a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which are the building blocks of web development. I have also delved into front-end frameworks like React, allowing me to create dynamic and interactive websites. Constantly staying up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in web development is essential for me. I am always eager to learn new coding techniques, explore different design principles, and improve my problem-solving abilities.
